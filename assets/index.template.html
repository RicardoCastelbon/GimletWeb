About us

Gimlet is a project started in 2020 in the middle of the Covid-19 pandem ic by the renowned mixologist Mihail Mihailov and his partner Alejandra Castelbon. Even in these difficult circumstances, they attracted customers from the entire region of Cartagena and surprise us with the quality of their products and service.

WHAT DO WE OFFER?

In gimlet you will experience a variety of cocktails, top quality organic wines and typical mediterranean products. Everything prepared with great passion and exquisite service.
